I’m a designer, educator, and many other things that make nice lists of three.

I love creating experiences that get people curious and engaged — and all of the learning, problem solving, and trust building that come along the way.

For over a decade, I’ve helped organizations explore creative challenges and develop new ways of connecting with their audiences. Together with design, some my favorite tools for this work are experimentation, collaboration, and playfulness.

When I’m offline, I enjoy observing the mundane, spinning up new projects, and studying the finer points of Spanish grammar.
